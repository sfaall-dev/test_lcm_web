Missions

The Joint Microbiology Laboratory (LCM) was created in 2004 following several tripartite agreements between the Research Institute for Development ( IRD https://ird.fr ), the Senegalese Institute of Agricultural Research ( ISRA https://isra.sn ) and Cheikh Anta Diop University ( UCAD https://ucad.sn ).

The laboratory's missions are summarized as follows:

  • Generate knowledge and exchange biological material and scientific information in the field of microbiology and microbial biotechnologies;
  • Develop scientific expertise and research training through research in the field of microbiology and microbial biotechnologies;
  • Provide training oriented towards the acquisition of the most recent conceptual and technological advances in microbiology and microbial biotechnologies;
  • Propose training focused on technological innovations and know-how in a sustainable development perspective;
  • Develop human resources in microbial biotechnology aiming to provide the country with high-level scientists according to its agricultural, agro-food and natural resource potentials.

The LCM is physically located at the Bel-Air Research Center, Route des hydrocarbures, Dakar Senegal. BP 1386. Tel: +33 849 33 26
